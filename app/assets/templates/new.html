What is Table For Eight?

Table For Eight is an interactive app that takes the hassle and stress out of planning a dining experience with your mates. Invite your friends, and they'll help you plan this out by voting their preferences later.


Sounds good? So how do I start? Just follow these 3 simple steps:

Who?
Friends! Who do you want to come? Add 'em to the guest list. Oh, and don't forget to tell them who you are!
Where?
Let's choose where to eat! List up to five restaurants.
When?
Sunday brunch, weekday lunch, or Friday dinner? Select up to three dates.
